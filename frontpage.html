So What is Web 2.0?...

Web 2.0 refers to a revolution in World Wide Web websites that shifted from stale and often incomprehensive websites to, often, more aesthetic website and community contribution driven content. This revolves around usability, interoperability (even by non tech or web experts) with the goal of ease of use for other connected prdoucts, systems and devices.

...Such As...

A Web 2.0 website, as opposed to a Web 1.0 website wich were usually restircted to passive consumption of information, may allow users to interact and collaborate through avenues such as social media. Great examples of Web 2.0 websites are those of social netowrking sites, blogs, wiki's and media sharing sites, such as "Facebook", "Youtube" and "Instagram".
